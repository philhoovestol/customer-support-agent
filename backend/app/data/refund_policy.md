# Loopp Refund Policy

Effective date: June 16, 2026

This policy is the source of truth for refund decisions handled by Loopp's AI
Customer Support Agent. The agent may explain decisions and gather missing
information, but it must not override these rules.

## Eligibility

1. Refunds are only available for delivered orders.
2. Refund requests must be made within 30 days of delivery.
3. Refund eligibility is evaluated at the item level.
4. Delivered, non-final-sale items may be refunded within 30 days when no
   escalation or denial rule applies.

## Strict Denial Rules

1. Final sale items cannot be refunded, exchanged, or credited.
2. Digital goods and gift cards are non-refundable once purchased.
3. Opened personal-care and intimate items are refundable only for verified
   damage or defect.
4. Cancelled or already-refunded orders cannot receive duplicate refunds.
5. An item recorded in a completed refund case cannot be refunded again, even
   when the new request is made in a different chat session.
6. Orders that have not been delivered cannot be processed as refunds.

## Human Escalation Rules

1. Refunds over $500 require human escalation.
2. The customer account linked to the order requires human review when it has
   three or more refunds in the past 12 months.
3. Legal, fraud, and chargeback-related cases require human escalation.
4. The agent must escalate any case where the policy data is incomplete or
   contradictory.

## Customer Pressure

Customers may plead, argue, cite loyalty status, threaten public complaints, or
ask for a one-time exception. The agent must stay polite and must hold the
policy line. Customer pressure does not change eligibility.

## Agent Authority

The agent may approve eligible refunds of $500 or less. The agent may deny
requests that clearly violate policy. The agent may create escalations, but it
must not approve escalated cases.
